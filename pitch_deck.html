SparkTutor — 5-minute personalised lessons (Extended)

Problem

Students need quick targeted help and teachers need an easy way to create shareable micro-lessons.

Solution

A PWA that recommends micro-lessons, offers templated explainers, allows teachers to author lessons locally, and supports accessible reading features.

New Features

  • Teacher authoring + encoded share links
  • Read Aloud TTS
  • Dark mode & Dyslexia font
  • Export progress CSV

Tech

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, localStorage for persistence, PWA manifest & service worker. All frontend—no servers required.

Impact & Roadmap

Enables offline-first teaching micro-content distribution; next: LLM explainers, cloud sync, teacher dashboards.
